Why one content strategy fails all three buyers

Imagine a cooking school with three types of students: a complete beginner, a professional restaurant chef, and a regional tourism board. If you send the same flyer to all three, none of them feels it was written for them ‚Äî and none of them enrolls. The beginner needs: "Cook family dinner in 3 months." The chef needs: "Elevate your plating to justify higher menu prices."
